About us

SHRI SATGURU DEVAI NAMAH
SHRI Anandpur Trust

SHRI PARAMHANS ADVAIT MATH made its advent over a hundred years ago to spread and preach Truth, ideals of Bhakti, Karam-Yoga, and spiritual knowledge. It was founded by the First Great Master, the Prophet of the age, PARAMHANS SHRI SHRI 108 SHRI SWAMI ADVAIT ANANDJI MAHARAJ, Sanyasi Puri born in 1846 A.D

tirelessly worked for the good of humanity and spiritual upliftment of the common masses. This holy mission was successfully carried out by his Sanyasi successors and is fastly moving towards its goal by bestowing lasting peace of mind and tranquility to the makind and promoting universal brotherhood, so essential in this age of greed and selfishness. Selfless service to the mankind is the motto of this Trust. The present FIFTH MASTER, SHRI SHRI 108 SHRI SWAMI DARSHAN PURAN ANANDJI MAHARAJ JI OF THE SAMPRADAYA, has accelerated the pace of promotion of spiritual knowledge and selfless service.
Shri Anandpur Trust, established in 1954, is located at P.O.Shri Anandpur near Esagarh, Distt. Ashoknagar (MP), is an embodiment of spirituality, social services, truth and love and it adorns the State of Madhya Pradesh by its precious higher ideals taught by our Rishis of ancient past. All these activities are in furtherance of the mission of Shri Paramhans Advait Mat for bestowing peace, love and joy in the lives of suffering humanity through thousands of Sadhus, Sanyasis and selfless workers.
This Trust has always done its duty towards the nation and risen to the occasion whenever emergencies and natural calamities demanded. The Trust has been contributing to the PM’s Relief Fund and Madhya Pradesh CM’s Relief Fund quite liberally. The Trust has also been assisting various Social and Relief Institutions in MP as also in other States for promotion of social causes for the benefit of the general masses. The Trust, being secular in its approach, is also engaged in carring out humanitarian services in the field of education and medical aid by instituting schools, charitable dispensaries and hospitals, where free medical and surgical treatment is imparted to a large number of poor and needy patients irrespective of caste, creed and religion.

About Diagnostic center

SHRI ANANDPUR TRUST gave a new dimension of high magnitude of service to humanity by constructing a high tech Charitable Diagnostic Centre which is spread over a sprawling 20,000sq.ft. area which started functioning w.e.f. 27-1-2004. This centre is the only one of its kind in the city NCR Delhi, where most of state-of-art Diagnostic Facilities are provided under one roof at a very reasonable cost.
